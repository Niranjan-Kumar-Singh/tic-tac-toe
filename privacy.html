Effective date: July 2, 2025

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on Tic Tac Toe Master. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Cookies and Log Files

This site follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. GDPR Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights.
