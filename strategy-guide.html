Always Start in the Center

Essential

The center gives you access to the most winning combinations. It maximizes your control over the game board and creates the foundation for multiple winning strategies. This move gives you 4 possible winning lines!

Prioritize Corners

Tactical

If the center is taken, go for a corner. This increases your chance of creating a fork (two ways to win) and puts pressure on your opponent's defensive play. Corners offer 3 winning lines each.
