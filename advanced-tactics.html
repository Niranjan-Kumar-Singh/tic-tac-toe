Control the Center

Essential

The center square is the most powerful position on the board, giving you access to 4 different winning lines (horizontal, vertical, and both diagonals). Always prioritize taking the center when going first ‚Äî it maximizes your winning opportunities and forces your opponent into reactive play.

Fork Strategy

Offensive

Create a fork by setting up two potential winning moves simultaneously. This deadly tactic forces your opponent to choose which threat to block, guaranteeing your victory on the next move. Master this and you'll win against most opponents who don't see it coming.

Block Their Forks

Defensive

Recognition is key! Watch for patterns where your opponent can create multiple threats. If you can't block both potential forks, create your own forcing threat to make them defend, disrupting their fork setup. Defense wins games!

Opposite Corner Play

Counter

When your opponent takes a corner and you're going second, claim the opposite corner. This strategic move limits their advantage and creates your own winning opportunities. It's a crucial counter-strategy that many casual players overlook.

Edge Strategy

Tactical

When center and corners are occupied, edge squares become tactical weapons. Use edges to block opponent threats while setting up your own traps. Smart edge play can turn a defensive position into a winning opportunity.
